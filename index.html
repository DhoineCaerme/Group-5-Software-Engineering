The Philosophy

What is Dialectical Reasoning?

Thesis

An initial proposition or argument is presented. The Advocate Agent builds the strongest possible case for a particular decision path.

Antithesis

A counter-proposition challenges the thesis. The Critic Agent identifies weaknesses, risks, and alternative perspectives.

Synthesis

A reconciliation emerges. The Mediator Agent integrates valid points from both sides into a refined, de-risked decision.

"The dialectic method is not merely a debate technique—it's a systematic approach to uncovering truth through the collision of opposing ideas."
— Inspired by Hegelian Dialectics
The Process

How the Framework Operates

1

Problem Ingestion

An ambiguous decision scenario is fed into the system with all relevant context and constraints.

2

Agent Initialization

Multiple specialized AI agents are instantiated with distinct reasoning personas and objectives.

3

Dialectical Rounds

Agents engage in structured debate rounds, each building upon previous arguments and counter-arguments.

4

Risk Extraction

Key risks, assumptions, and blindspots are systematically identified and categorized.

5

Synthesis Generation

A final synthesized recommendation emerges with explicit risk mitigations and confidence levels.
